Bee v2.9 focuses on connectivity, payload flexibility, and transaction stability under load, while addressing storage bottlenecks.

* libp2p websocket channels for direct real-time communication  
* Support for multiple GSOC payloads and subscription availability
* Extended reserve size for node operators
* Multiple aggregators retrieving all payloads
* Gas fee limit and retry mechanisms  
* LevelDB bottleneck fixes
